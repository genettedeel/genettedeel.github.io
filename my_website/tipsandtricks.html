Tips and Tricks!

Scratch

1. A very important tip to remember is to save your work ALOT!

2. Work with your partner and build ideas off of each other.

3. It can be stressful if a program isn't working, but don't delete everything right away.

4. Don't take the easy way out, keep challenging yourself and skills.

5. Try to make games that are different from the last one you made.


Cloud 9

1. Use examples to get ideas.

2. Troubleshoot with your table partner.

3. If nothing changed on your website after making changes, remember to click "run" and refresh.

4. Decorate with CSS and make your website exciting.
